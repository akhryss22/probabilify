About The Project

A Research Paper Submitted to the Mapúa Senior High School Department in Partial Fulfillment of the Requirements for Practical Research 2 (RES02).

Our team is developing Probabilify, an adaptive e-learning system that personalizes Statistics and Probability learning experiences based on MBTI personality types.
